Our Campus

Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur (IIIT-NR), is a University established by the ‘International Institute of Information Technology University Act, 2013’ of the Government of Chhattisgarh, is a joint venture of Chhattisgarh State Government and NTPC. The institute is committed to pursue excellence in higher education, research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines. It firmly believes in bestowing knowledge dissemination and imparting entrepreneurial skills to the students to enable them to address real world problems. To empower this vision, IIIT-NR provides state of-the-art research and education facilities and nurtures a campus culture that fosters high energy and enthusiasm in every individual, clubbed with highest standard of professionalism.

The twentyfive acres fully residential campus of IIIT-NR is located in “the Born Smart City of Naya Raipur”, approximately 23 Kms. from the city of Raipur and 15 minutes’ drive from Swami Vivekananda Airport, Raipur. The IIIT-NR campus is enabled with Wi-Fi connectivity, CCTV surveillance, hi-tech security measures and excellent amenities for sports and other recreational activities. The modern architecture of the buildings and the serene environment of the campus foster an ambience for the students for creativity and innovation. The classrooms and laboratories are equipped with state-of-the-art technologies for teaching and learning. IIIT-NR aims to emerge as India’s prime contributor for innovation and research, and to foster technical talent of global standards.

OBJECTIVES AND SCOPE OF THE PROPOSED INSTITUTION

1. Objectives of the Institution

The institute is committed to pursue excellence in higher education, research and development in Information Technology (IT) and associated disciplines.

2. General and Technical Education Scenario of the State

The total numbers of universities in the state are more than ten (including 7 State universities, 1 central university, 6 private universities, 204 Government colleges and 256 private colleges) providing technical education in engineering and associated disciplines.

3. Status at Entry Level

IIIT-NR was established as a University by the Govt. of Chhattisgarh in 2015 and recognized by UGC as a State University in 2018. The institute campus is enabled with modern amenities and equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and technologies for teaching and learning. IIIT-NR currently offers 3 B.Tech. programmes in Electronics & Communication Engineeing (ECE), Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) apart from two M.Tech. programmes and Doctoral programme (Ph.D.) in ECE, CSE, Physics, Mathematics, English & Management.
