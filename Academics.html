Our Academic Programs

IIIT-NR fosters excellence in learning, research, scholarship and innovation, and that helps create leaders in the fields of Communication and information technology. Clearly, our endeavor is to go beyond the typical institute pedagogy practices in India. We emphasize on “Skill first, Theory later and Project integrated” approach to pedagogy thus encouraging curiosity, creativity and problem solving abilities. Our undergraduate and research programmes are built on the above principles where experiment and innovation meet eye-to eye. The dynamic structure of the curriculum orchestrates with the changing needs of industry and academia.
