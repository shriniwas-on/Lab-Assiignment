B.Tech Admission 2022

Admission to the undergraduate program of the DSPM IIIT Naya Raipur is based strictly on the merit of the candidate as inferred from their performance in JEE (Main). The eligibility for applying for the B. Tech program under the Chhattisgarh quota at IIIT Naya Raipur is 50% aggregate marks in Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics (45% for SC/ST/OBC/PWD candidates) in their 12th standard examination. The eligibility for applying for the B. Tech program under the All India category and NTPC quota is as per JoSAA guidelines.

  • 35% of seats are reserved for all India categories (Please visit JoSAA portal for applying for the same).
  • 15% of seats are reserved for people employed with NTPC or their wards.
  • 50% of seats are reserved for students who have passed 10th & 12th from the State of Chhattisgarh.
